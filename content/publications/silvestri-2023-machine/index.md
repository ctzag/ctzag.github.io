abstract: 'Digitization in healthcare, through Electronic Health Records and connected medical devices, has improved care delivery but sharply increased cyber threats, with system vulnerabilities a key source of risk. This paper proposes a machine-learning approach — using a BERT neural language model and XGBoost — to extract and assess cyber threats and vulnerabilities from natural-language documents freely available on the web, and to estimate their severity level for healthcare systems. Experiments on cybersecurity news and CVE vulnerability reports demonstrate that the approach provides a realistic way to assess threats and vulnerabilities from text, suitable for adoption in real-world healthcare ecosystems.'
